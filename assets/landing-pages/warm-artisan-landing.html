Our Story

From our hands to your home

What began in a small garage studio in 2016 has grown into a passionate practice of mindful making. We believe in the beauty of imperfection, the value of slow craft, and the connection that forms between maker and keeper.

Every piece that leaves our studio carries with it countless hours of intention—from the sourcing of local clays to the final glaze firing.

— Emma & James

The Craft

How we make

1

Sourcing

We begin with locally-sourced clay and natural materials from Pacific Northwest suppliers.

2

Shaping

Each piece is thrown on the wheel or hand-built, taking shape over several sessions.

3

Glazing

We mix our own glazes using traditional recipes and apply them by hand.

4

Firing

Two firings bring each piece to life—bisque first, then a final glaze firing.
