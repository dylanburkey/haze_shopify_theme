# Product Overview

**Forge Industrial** is a premium Shopify theme designed specifically for manufacturing, industrial, and B2B e-commerce businesses. The theme targets the $320 price point on the Shopify Theme Store.

## Target Market
- Manufacturers and distributors
- Industrial B2B brands
- Technical product companies
- Equipment suppliers

## Key Value Proposition
"Everything you need, no apps required" - The theme eliminates expensive third-party apps by including native features like loyalty systems, advanced SEO, PWA support, and dynamic grid builders.

## Primary Use Case
Zebra Skimmers (oil skimmers and coolant automation systems) serves as the primary development reference, informing the industrial focus and technical specification requirements.

## Core Features
- Technical specification display for complex products
- Product comparison functionality
- Trust indicators (warranties, certifications, "Made in USA")
- Professional B2B conversion-optimized experience
- Preset system with 12 industry-specific configurations
- Section groups for swappable headers/footers
- Container query-based responsive layouts

## Built-In Features (No Apps Required)
The theme includes features that typically require expensive monthly app subscriptions:

- **Loyalty Program** ($50-200/mo value) - Tier-based rewards with progress tracking
- **Advanced SEO** ($20-50/mo value) - JSON-LD structured data and rich snippets
- **Mega Menu** ($15-30/mo value) - Multi-column navigation with featured content
- **Advanced FAQ** ($10-25/mo value) - Searchable FAQ with categories
- **Product Attachments** ($15-40/mo value) - Manuals, specs, CAD files
- **PWA Support** ($20-60/mo value) - Installable app experience

**Total Value: $150-400/month in app savings**
