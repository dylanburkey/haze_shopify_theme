# Header & Footer Variants Guide

This theme includes multiple header and footer styles that you can easily swap in the Shopify theme editor—no code required.

---

## Available Header Variants

| Variant | Best For | File |
|---------|----------|------|
| **Standard Header** | General stores, clean aesthetic | `header.liquid` |
| **Mega Menu Header** | Large catalogs, complex navigation | `mega-menu-header.liquid` |
| **Industrial Header** | B2B, manufacturing, equipment | `header-industrial.liquid` |
| **Corporate Header** | Premium brands, artisan products | `corporate-header.liquid` |

## Available Footer Variants

| Variant | Best For | File |
|---------|----------|------|
| **Standard Footer** | General use, works with most headers | `footer.liquid` |
| **Industrial Footer** | Pairs with Industrial Header | `footer-industrial.liquid` |
| **Preset Footer** | Enhanced styling for Bold Impact/Tech Forward | `preset-footer.liquid` |

---

## How to Swap Headers or Footers

### Step 1: Open the Theme Editor
1. Go to **Online Store → Themes**
2. Click **Customize** on your active theme

### Step 2: Navigate to Header/Footer Group
1. In the left sidebar, look for **Header group** or **Footer group**
2. Click to expand the group

### Step 3: Remove Current Section
1. Click on the current header (e.g., "Header")
2. Scroll to the bottom of the settings panel
3. Click **Remove section**

### Step 4: Add New Variant
1. Click **Add section** within the group
2. Browse or search for your desired variant (e.g., "Mega Menu Header")
3. Click to add it

### Step 5: Configure & Save
1. Adjust any settings for the new header/footer
2. Click **Save** in the top right

## Tips

- **Preview before publishing**: Always preview your changes on different pages before saving
- **Mobile check**: Test how headers look on mobile—mega menus collapse to hamburger menus
- **Announcement bar**: The announcement bar works with all header variants
- **Section groups**: You can only have one header and one footer active at a time

---

## Troubleshooting

**Header/footer not showing?**
- Make sure the section is added to the correct group (Header group or Footer group)
- Check that no other header/footer sections exist in the group

**Styling looks off?**
- Some variants work better with specific color presets
- Try switching to a recommended pairing above

**Can't find a variant?**
- Use the search in "Add section" 
- All variants have "Header" or "Footer" in their names
